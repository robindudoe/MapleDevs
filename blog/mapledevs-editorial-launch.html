Introducing The Maple Feed

MapleDevs Editorial is adding practical coverage for Canadian game developers, studios, students, and job seekers.

By MapleDevs Editorial The Maple Feed

The Maple Feed is the editorial home for MapleDevs: practical updates for people looking for work, hiring talent, or tracking Canada's game development scene.

What we will cover

Expect short, useful articles about Canadian studio hiring patterns, student-friendly roles, portfolio signals, regional job markets, and how to read listings with more confidence.

How it connects to the job board

Editorial posts will live beside the job board, but they will stay separate from scraped listings. That keeps the board clean while giving job seekers more context around the market.

Every article is published through the MapleDevs template, saved as a flat page inside the blog folder, and linked only after the file exists.

MapleDevs publishes practical editorial coverage for Canada's game development community.
