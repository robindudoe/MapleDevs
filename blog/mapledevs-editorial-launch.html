Introducing The Maple Feed

MapleDevs Editorial|April 22, 2026

Welcome to the first edition of The Maple Feed. This is a practical note for the Canadian game development community: what is changing, who is hiring, and where job seekers and studios should pay attention.

Our Mission

We built MapleDevs to cut through the noise of US-centric job boards. The Maple Feed is the next step in that work. The goal is simple: give Canadian game developers clearer context, whether you are a junior looking for your first role or a lead developer comparing opportunities across studios.

What to Expect

Expect short, useful updates: hiring notes, studio context, and practical guidance for Canadian game industry careers. We will keep the feed focused on verified Canadian opportunities and avoid filler.
